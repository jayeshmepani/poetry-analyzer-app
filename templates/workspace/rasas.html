Understanding Rasa Theory

What is Rasa?

Rasa (रस) means "essence" - the aesthetic pleasure derived from art. Established by Bharata in Natyashastra (2nd century BCE).

The 8 + 1 = 9 Rasas

Originally 8 rasas, expanded to 9 with Shanta (peace) - the ultimate goal of arts leading to spiritual liberation.

How it's evoked

Through Vibhavas (causes), Anubhavas (effects), and Vyabhichari Bhavas (transitory states).
