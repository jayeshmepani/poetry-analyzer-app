English Poetic Forms

Sonnet

14 lines, iambic pentameter

Variants: Petrarchan (ABBAABBA CDECDE), Shakespearean (ABAB CDCD EFEF GG)

Villanelle

19 lines, 5 tercets + 1 quatrain, 2 refrains

Form: A1bA2 abA1 abA2 abA1 abA2 aA1A2

Haiku

3 lines, 5-7-5 syllable pattern

Japanese origin, nature themes

Limerick

5 lines, anapestic, AABBA rhyme

Humorous, 9-9-6-6-9 syllable pattern

Free Verse

No fixed meter or rhyme

Modern, organic structure

Blank Verse

Unrhymed iambic pentameter

Shakespeare, Milton

Terza Rima

ABA BCB CDC... chain rhyme

Dante's Divine Comedy

Hindi Poetic Forms (छंद)

दोहा (Doha)

24 matras (13+11) per couplet

Kabir, Rahim - didactic

चौपाई (Chaupai)

16 matras per line, 4 lines

Ramcharitmanas - narrative

सोरठा (Soratha)

13+11 matras, mirror of Doha

Folk tradition

कुंडलिया (Kundaliya)

24 matras × 6 charan

Chain-linked, same word start/end

रोला (Rola)

24 matras (11+13), 4 lines

Paired rhyme, narrative

कवित्त/सवैया (Kavitt/Savaiya)

31-33 varnas per charan

Ornate court poetry

हरिगीતિકા (Harigitika)

28 matras per charan

Yati after 16, ends लघु-गुरु

બર્વૈ (Barvai)

12+7 matras per line

Even charan ends with जगण

Urdu Poetic Forms (اشعار)

غزل (Ghazal)

AA BA CA DA... minimum 5 couplets

Beher, Qaafiya (rhyme), Radif (refrain)

نظم (Nazm)

Unified theme, flexible structure

Modern Urdu poetry

ربعی (Rubai)

4 lines, AAAA or AABA

Quatrain form

مثنوی (Masnavi)

Long narrative poem, AA BB CC

Epic length

اردو (氵شعار)

Couplet form

Classical verse

રાસ (Raas)

પદ્યબંધ (Padyabandh)
